Overview

Space Type: Single-story residential space
Space Size: 35-40 ping / 115.70265 - 132.2314 (m²)
Space Layout/Interior Scenes:

  1. Bedroom
  2. Bathroom
  3. Kitchen & Dining
  4. Living Room
  5. Home Office (Study)
  6. Hallway & Balcony: Scenic balcony and work balcony (included in the area)

Space Orientation: North-South facing
Space Path/Script: Anticipated/expected spatial user's flow within the space (checkpoint)
User's Perspective: Looking to rent/buy a home for living, evaluating the property (2 adult, 2 children)
Developer's Perspective: Selling a property, highlighting essential locations/scenes
Architectural Design Variation Parameters: A total of 4 parameters, each with 2 conditions, resulting in 2x2x2x2=16 variations.
Recruiting 4 designers, each responsible for one set of 4 designs.
